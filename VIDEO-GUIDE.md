## VIDEO SPECIFICATIONS

### Video Files (.mp4):
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
- **Duration:** 30-90 seconds (short product showcases work best)
- **Resolution:** 
  - Minimum: 720p (1280x720)
  - Recommended: 1080p (1920x1080)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 9:16 (portrait/vertical) or 3:4
- **File Size:** Under 50MB per video (compress if needed)
- **Frame Rate:** 24-30 fps

### Thumbnail Images (.jpg):
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Size:** 600x800px (portrait orientation)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 3:4 (portrait)
- **File Size:** Under 200KB each
- **Quality:** High quality, clear preview of video content

---

## HOW TO ADD VIDEOS

### Step 1: Prepare Your Videos
1. Record or collect product videos (showcasing products, packaging, quality, etc.)
2. Edit videos to 30-90 seconds
3. Export as MP4 format
4. Compress if file size is over 50MB

### Step 2: Create Thumbnails
1. Take a screenshot from each video (best frame)
2. Crop to portrait orientation (3:4 ratio)
3. Save as JPG
4. Optimize file size

### Step 3: Rename Files
Rename your files exactly as listed:
- Videos: `product-video-1.mp4`, `product-video-2.mp4`, etc.
- Thumbnails: `video-thumb-1.jpg`, `video-thumb-2.jpg`, etc.

### Step 4: Upload Files
- Place video files in: `assets/videos/`
- Place thumbnail images in: `assets/images/`

### Step 5: Test
1. Open your website
2. Scroll to the Product Videos section
3. Videos should show thumbnails
4. Click play to test video playback
